Aether - The Traveler

Anemo Element | Main Character

Story

Aether is the male protagonist of Genshin Impact and one of two possible travelers. He arrives in Teyvat as an outsider, searching for his lost sibling. Throughout his journey, Aether learns to harness the power of the Anemo element and meets many allies who help him uncover the mysteries of this world.

Despite being a stranger to this land, Aether's determination and kindness have made him a beloved hero. His story is one of discovery, friendship, and the quest to reunite with what he lost. As he explores the world, he becomes increasingly entangled in the fate of Teyvat itself.

Weapons

Aether's preferred weapons for combat:

Skyward Blade

A legendary sword that cuts through the air itself, dealing massive damage with its elegant strikes.

Primordial Jade Cutter

A mystical blade forged in jade, increasing both attack and HP when equipped.

Aquila Favonia

A powerful wind sword that grants physical damage bonus and deals wind damage in bursts.

Favorite Hobby

Aether's favorite hobby is exploring and adventuring. He has an insatiable curiosity about the world of Teyvat and enjoys discovering new landscapes, meeting interesting characters, and uncovering hidden secrets. Whether it's climbing mountains, swimming through rivers, or delving into ancient ruins, Aether finds joy in the journey itself.

When not on adventures, Aether also enjoys cooking meals with his companions. He takes pride in creating delicious dishes that bring people together, viewing food as a way to strengthen bonds with those he cares about.
