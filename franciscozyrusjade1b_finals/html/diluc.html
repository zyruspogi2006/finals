Diluc - The Dark knignt Hero

Pyro Element | 5-Star DPS

Story

As the owner of the Dawn Winery, known across Mondstadt as a stern and relentless protector of the city. Cold and distant on the surface, he is deeply driven by a strong sense of justice shaped by past loss and disillusionment with the Knights of Favonius. In combat, Diluc excels as a powerful main DPS, chaining fiery claymore strikes with Pyro-infused skills to overwhelm enemies. His dark, brooding demeanor contrasts with his quiet care for Mondstadt, making him a classic lone-wolf hero with a fiery edge.

Weapons

Diluc's preferred weapons for combat:

Skyward Pride

A greatsword that unleashes wind currents, perfect for Diluc's fiery attacks.

Wolf's Gravestone

A legendary greatsword that increases attack power and damage bonus significantly.

Serpent Spine

A claymore that increases crit rate and rewards aggressive playstyles with higher damage.

Favorite Hobby

Diluc's favorite hobby is managing the Dawn Winery. Despite his role as a vigilante, he takes great pride in running the winery and producing fine wines. The work helps him unwind from his nightly activities and provides him with a sense of normalcy.

When not occupied with winery duties or his duties as the Darknight Hero, Diluc enjoys training and perfecting his combat techniques. He is a perfectionist who constantly seeks to improve his skills to better protect Mondstadt. He also has a subtle soft side and occasionally enjoys peaceful moments watching sunsets over his vineyard.
