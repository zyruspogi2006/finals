Hu Tao - The Pyro Specialist

Pyro Element | 5-Star DPS

Story

Hu Tao is the eccentric director of the Wangsheng Funeral Parlor. Her playful demeanor hides a deep respect for life and death, and she channels Pyro energy with intensity.

Her methods are unorthodox but effective, and she values tradition while embracing the cycle of life.

Weapons

Hu Tao's preferred weapons for combat:

Staff of Homa

A polearm that increases HP and scales attack with max HP.

Dragon's Bane

A polearm that increases damage against enemies affected by Hydro or Pyro.

Favorite Hobby

Hu Tao enjoys storytelling and performing rituals at the funeral parlor. She keeps a balance between life and death with her duties and quirky personality.
