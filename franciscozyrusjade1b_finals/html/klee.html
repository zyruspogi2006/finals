Klee - The Spark Knight

Pyro Element | 5-Star DPS

Story

Klee is a cheerful, mischievous child from Mondstadt who serves as the “Spark Knight” of the Knights of Favonius and is famous for her love of explosives and playful curiosity. Her story highlights a bright, adventurous personality that often gets her into trouble but endears her to friends and guardians.


Her energetic nature brings joy to those around her, but her antics sometimes get her in trouble. Loyal and innocent, Klee's pyrotechnics are as effective as they are adorable.
