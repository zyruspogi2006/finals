Noelle - The Gentle Knight

Geo Element | 4-Star Tank/Support

Story

devoted maid of the Knights of Favonius who dreams of one day becoming a full knight. Kind-hearted, earnest, and almost comically hardworking, she takes on any task—no matter how dangerous or exhausting—with unwavering determination and a smile. In battle, Noelle excels as a durable all-rounder, using her Geo abilities to create shields, heal her team through her attacks, and deal heavy claymore damage, especially when her burst is active. Her strength, both physical and emotional, contrasts sweetly with her polite and gentle nature, making her one of Mondstadt's most reliable and endearing characters.

Weapons

Noelle's preferred weapons for combat:

Whiteblind

A claymore that boosts DEF and attack when attacking.

Serpent Spine

A claymore that rewards aggressive play.

Favorite Hobby

Noelle’s favorite hobbies are cleaning and tidying (she takes pride in her maid duties), practicing combat and polishing armor as she trains to become a Knight, helping and caring for others around Mondstadt, and cooking—especially making pancakes
