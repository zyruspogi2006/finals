Raiden Shogun - The Electro Archon

Electro Element | 5-Star Archon

Story

The Raiden Shogun is the electro Archon and the supreme ruler of Inazuma. Known as the "Raiden Ei," she has ruled the Inazuma Shogunate for thousands of years with unwavering resolve. Her dream of "Eternity" drives all her actions, though the pursuit of this ideal has created conflict across her domain.

Despite her stoic and formal demeanor, the Raiden Shogun possesses great strength and wisdom accumulated over millennia. She is a master of the blade and commands electro powers with devastating precision. Her journey involves balancing her vision of eternity with the changing desires of her people and allies.

Weapons

Raiden Shogun's preferred weapons for combat:

Engulfing Lightning

The signature weapon of Raiden Shogun, greatly increasing her energy recharge and burst damage.

The Catch

A polearm that increases burst damage and energy recovery, perfect for elemental burst builds.

Lithic Spear

A polearm that grows stronger the more Liyue characters are in the party, offering increased ATK.

Favorite Hobby

Raiden Shogun's favorite hobby is meditating in the Plane of Euthymia, a realm she created within her mind. In this sacred space, she contemplates her decisions and seeks deeper understanding of the nature of eternity and change.

When not meditating, the Raiden Shogun enjoys traditional Inazuman arts such as poetry and calligraphy. She appreciates the discipline required in these practices, viewing them as expressions of her philosophy. She also takes great pleasure in testing her combat skills against worthy opponents, constantly refining her already formidable abilities.
