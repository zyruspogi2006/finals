Zhongli - The Geo Support

Geo Element | 5-Star Support

Story

Zhongli is a calm and dignified consultant who holds ancient wisdom and knowledge. As the Geo Archon in mortal form, he values tradition and order.

His mastery of geo powers makes him a stalwart protector and one of the most respected figures in Liyue.

Weapons

Zhongli's preferred weapons for combat:

Vortex Vanquisher

A polearm that increases shield strength and attack.

Staff of Homa

A polearm that increases HP and attack scaling.

Favorite Hobby

Zhongli enjoys tea ceremonies and reminiscing about Liyue's history. He often reflects on the passage of time and the meaning of contracts.
